📋 How to Print at Staples

  1. Print to PDF: Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac)
  2. Select "Save as PDF" as your printer
  3. Save as: BlackHorse-BusinessCard.pdf
  4. Go to staples.com → Print Services → Business Cards
  5. Upload your PDF
  6. Select options:
    • Quantity: 500 or 1000
    • Size: 3.5" × 2"
    • Paper: Standard (or Glossy for premium look)
  7. Review proof → Order

💡 Pro tip: Test print one copy at home first to verify colors & sizing before ordering 500+.
